## Section 5: The "Pre-Flight" Submission Checklist (Objection Handling)

Before you submit your music to a record label, run through the standard industry filters. LabelScout automatically checks these parameters in your SoundCloud link, so you pass the first-stage A&R filter:

1. **Mixed & Mastered Standards**: Is your track sonically competitive with current releases? Labels discard up to 60% of demos due to poor audio quality [11][12].
2. **Private SoundCloud Etiquette**: Is your track set to "Private" with the secret token active [11]? If an A&R sees a public link or a track with 200 previous plays, they assume other labels have already passed on it [4][9].
3. **Download Permissions Enabled**: Did you enable downloads on your SoundCloud link [6]? A&Rs need to download 320kbps MP3s to test your tracks on CDJs or studio monitors [4][6].
4. **No Attachments, Ever**: Never attach audio files directly to email pitches. It clogs inboxes and gets flagged as spam [11].

## Section 6: FAQ (Objection Handling & Trust Building)

#### Q: Do you submit my music directly to major record labels?

**A**: No, and you shouldn't either. Major labels (Sony, Universal, Warner) explicitly enforce strict, legally mandated "no unsolicited material" policies to avoid copyright lawsuits ([1][8]). They only accept submissions through music attorneys or established managers. LabelScout helps you match and pitch to **highly active, independent boutique labels** where direct, high-quality submissions have a realistic signing rate [9].

#### Q: How does the AI matching algorithm work?

**A**: When you submit your reference artists, our engine queries the Spotify API to pull their recent discography and inspects the copyright metadata (the "P-line") to find the exact labels behind those releases [6]. We then run an automated web-scraper (using Perplexity) to search each label's web footprint to verify if they are active, check their current demo submission channels, and pull the name of their lead A&R person [8].

#### Q: What if a label's submission email or link is outdated?

**A**: A&R turnover is high. That is why LabelScout does not rely on a static, outdated database. Our system executes **live, programmatic web searches** using AI-agent nodes to find the most current guidelines posted on their website or social media Linktrees before generating your report ([5][8]).

#### Q: Is my unreleased music safe and secure?

**A**: Absolutely. LabelScout never downloads, stores, or publicly shares your audio files. We only analyze the text-based link and metadata you provide to evaluate the URL's formatting correctness, keeping your unreleased intellectual property completely private.
